Our Story


Welcome to TheClassTracker!



Created by University of South Florida alumni who understand the need students have for a fast, reliable tracking service. Our team has created a tracking solution that gives users an edge to get into their desired sections with a particular professor or at a particular time. With transparent pricing at a nominal $2.00 per section, TheClassTracker aims to make class tracking simple and accessible to all students.



Why Use TheClassTracker?

Tired of missing out on the classes you need? Class Tracker is here to help! This simple, reliable service keeps you updated on course availability at the University of South Florida, so you can focus on planning your semester without the stress of constant checking.

  • Track Seats: Select the classes you're interested in, and TheClassTracker will monitor seat availability.
  • Get Notified: Be the first to know when a seat opens up! We'll keep sending you notifications as long as a section remains open to help grab your attention. Once a section is closed again, these notifications will stop.
  • Register with Confidence: Once notified, it's up to you to register through the University's official system.
