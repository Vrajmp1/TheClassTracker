Our Story


Welcome to TheClassTracker!



Created by University of South Florida alumni who understand the need students have for a fast, reliable tracking service. Our team has created a tracking solution that gives users an edge to get into their desired sections with a particular professor or at a particular time.



What is TheClassTracker?


  • Track Seats: Select the classes you're interested in, and TheClassTracker will monitor seat availability.
  • Get Notified: Be the first to know when a seat opens up! We'll keep sending you notifications as long as a section remains open to help grab your attention. Once a section is closed again, these notifications will stop.
  • Register with Confidence: Once notified, it's up to you to register through the University's official system.

    What makes TheClassTracker unique?


  • Transparent pricing structure: Other course-tracking platforms typically offer the first tracked course for free and then charge to upgrade the account to premium to track "unlimited courses." Truth is - no one needs to track unlimited courses! However, these platforms often send delayed messages to free-tier users in order to give their paying users an advantage. With TheClassTracker, our low per section pricing model allows us to remain both competitive and transparent and send priority messages to all our users while ensuring fairness to those serious about using the service.
  • Minimalist and reliable design:We've focused on the core tracking functionality and built simplicity into our design which allows us to operate efficiently with minimal costs to students. Specifically, our service is offered as a chatbot through Telegram. By leveraging Telegram's existing infrastructure, the service requires no additional downloads, offering seamless cross-platform compatibility and providing instant integration with a familiar interface. No login, no accounts, just open Telegram and start tracking!
  • Speedy notifications: We've optimized our querying algorithm to account for the number of users tracking a specific section and the total number of users tracking sections within particular departments, enabling us to deliver faster updates through the Telegram API compared to traditional app-based solutions.
