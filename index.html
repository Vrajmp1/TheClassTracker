About Us

Our Story

Welcome to The Class Tracker!



Created by computer science students at the University of South Florida who were tired of relying on Coursicle for their tracking needs. After a year of hard work, we have created a tracking solution that gives user an edge to get into their desired sections for a particular professor or a particular time.



Why Choose Us?

Tired of missing out on the classes you need? Class Tracker is here to help! Our simple, reliable service keeps you updated on course availability at the University of South Florida, so you can focus on planning your semester without the stress of constant checking.
